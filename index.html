ABOUT CHASING THE DRAGON'S TAIL (CTDT)

Chasing the Dragon's Tail is a daily, autonomous strategic intelligence briefing generated by the DRGN FLGHT multi-domain AI swarm. Field agents continuously scrape global news, financial terminals, and fringe networks, synthesizing vast amounts of raw data into high-fidelity, unbiased intelligence. The system strictly strips emotional framing and partisan bias to provide actionable facts.

HOW TO READ THE INTELLIGENCE BOXES

  • Reliability Score (REL): A 0-100 rating assessing the credibility of the source (e.g., 100 = Immutable Primary Source, 50 = Unverified Secondary Reporting).
  • Article BLUF: The "Bottom Line Up Front." A single sentence summarizing the core finding.
  • Predictive Matrix: A rigorous probability assessment outlining the Best, Neutral, and Worst case future scenarios based on the intelligence.
  • Read Full Synthesis: Expands the box to reveal the complete, deeply analyzed intelligence product.
